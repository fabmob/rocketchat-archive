We found four actions to take ranked by importance from our brainstorming sessions Use an online forum or other tools to enable greater collaboration Find partners who have an interest in participating in the project Participate or organize hackathons to develop concepts and MVPs Engage universities in the project Which tools and processes should we use to ensure all stakeholders are aligned within this project ? File-sharing and collaboration, we will continue to use our google drive folder, please contact me to get write access. Blogging, we will continue to use our medium blog Event planning, we will continue to use our meetup group The tools for knowledge sharing and community discussions are important and before making a decision, I will also try and gather inputs at a conference next week called Open 2019 where people might have insights on this.
